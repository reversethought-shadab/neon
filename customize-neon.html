What's in the kit?

Our sign is made to enjoy straight from the box!

Every neon sign is mounted on clear acrylic for support and comes attached with a power cord that is to be attached to the power adaptor. The acrylic board has pre-drilled holes with SS mounting screws that allow you to hang it on your wall with ease.

Here's how you can install our neon signs on your wall:

Take a measuring tape and mark out the position of your neon sign.

Drill small holes on the wall.

Use the SS mounting screws to mount your neon sign on the wall.

Connect the power adapter to the transparent cable and your sign is ready!
